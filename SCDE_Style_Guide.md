# SCDE Style Guide for Claude
**South Carolina Department of Education — Office of Communications**

This guide gives Claude the brand rules needed to produce consistent, on-brand SCDE materials.

---

## Organization Identity

**Full name:** South Carolina Department of Education (SCDE)
**Office responsible for brand:** Office of Communications
**Design contact:** Nicole Arndt, Visual/Digital Media Director — nmarndt@ed.sc.gov | 803-734-8205

### Vision
> All students graduate prepared for success in college, careers, and citizenship. By 2030, districts will have available a system of personalized and digital learning that supports students in a safe learning environment to meet the Profile of the South Carolina Graduate.

### Mission
> The mission of the South Carolina Department of Education is to provide leadership and support so that all public education students graduate prepared for success.

### Tagline
> **Serve Students. Support Teachers. Empower Parents.**

Use the tagline verbatim. Do not paraphrase or reorder the three clauses.

---

## Logo

### History & Symbolism
- Founded **1868**
- Features South Carolina's **Palmetto tree** (symbol of resilience and hope) rooted in the pages of a **book** (education as foundation)
- Balances state heritage with forward momentum

### Logo Versions

| Version | Preferred Use |
|---|---|
| **Horizontal Stacked** | Primary — use whenever space allows |
| **Seal** | Smaller spaces |
| **Text Only** | Smallest spaces |

### Logo Rules (Do Not Violate)
1. Always place the logo over a **solid background** to maintain accessibility
2. Do **not** manipulate the logo into an unapproved format
3. Do **not** rotate the logo
4. Do **not** apply effects to the logo (unless explicitly permitted)
5. Do **not** place the logo on a conflicting or busy background

> Questions about logo use? Contact Nicole Arndt: nmarndt@ed.sc.gov

## Photography Style

### Guiding Principles
- **Emotional over aspirational** — capture candid, real moments; convey inspiration, not idealism
- **Authentic** — real people, believable body language; reject overly posed or staged images
- **Lived-in environments** — comfortable, casual, relaxed settings; avoid stark or artificial backgrounds; use lifestyle spaces that reflect reality

### Diversity
Diversity is a **top priority**. Photography and video must reflect the dynamic nature of SCDE's people, communities, and operations. Strive for inclusive representation across every image selection.

---

## Social Media

### Profile Photos
- Must match the SCDE logo as closely as possible (size, colors, style, variation)
- Must be **identical across all social media networks**
- Must scale down well — it appears small next to posts and comments

### Recommended Image Post Dimensions

| Platform Context | Dimensions |
|---|---|
| Standard landscape post | 1200px × 630px |
| Square post | 1080px × 1080px |
| Small / thumbnail | 420px × 220px |
| Wide portrait | 950px × 630px |

Optimize images for each network's specifications, especially when the same image is used across multiple platforms.
